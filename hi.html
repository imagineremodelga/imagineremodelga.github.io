FAQ

Common Questions

How much does a bathroom remodel cost in Snellville, GA? +

Bathroom remodel costs in Snellville typically range from $3,000 to $15,000 depending on the scope of work, materials, and fixtures chosen. We offer free detailed written estimates so you know exactly what to expect before we start.

Do you work on commercial properties? +

Yes! We serve both residential and commercial clients across Gwinnett County and Metro Atlanta. From office repaints to full commercial flooring installations, we handle projects of all sizes.

What types of flooring do you install? +

We install hardwood, luxury vinyl plank (LVP), laminate, and tile flooring. We help you choose the best option for your space, lifestyle, and budget.

How long does an interior paint job take? +

Most interior paint jobs take 1–3 days depending on the size of the space. We prep thoroughly, protect your floors and furniture, and leave the space spotless when finished.

Do you offer free estimates? +

Absolutely — all estimates are 100% free and come with a detailed written breakdown. You can book a walkthrough directly on our website using our online scheduler.
